What the flow covers

A complete learner journey playbook from lead drop to registration: the Golden Rule of Understand, Reflect, Counsel, Invite, Close, anchored on the 6 Cs mindset. It standardises first-call openings and probing, a structured DNP and follow-up cadence, objection handling, walk-in and video-call counselling, and enrolment closure.

The intent is to shift ACs perspective from telecallers to consultative selling, where learner success is prioritised and the habits of the best ACs become everyone's baseline. The companion Lead Nurture Flow maps the parallel connected and DNP paths, with the right WhatsApp collateral at every stage.

QA Auditing In progress Owner : Jay

Timeline
19 MayRequest raised to the quality team
1 JunFirst QA member hired and started training
15 JunSecond hire about to close, parallel thread initiated

Audit scope : four focus areas

  • Learner journey audits : all outreach via Ameyo plus WhatsApp collateral, accurate info per training, genuinely consultative counselling, walk-ins actively encouraged.
  • CRM hygiene : every call, video call, walk-in and WhatsApp follow-up logged accurately and promptly in LSQ. No missing entries.
  • VC recordings and call logs : all video calls recorded and logged, non-negotiable. Personal-phone or WhatsApp calls backed by screenshots and proofs in CRM.
  • Quality of communication : right information at the right time, referrers pitched on the tool, no counselling shortcuts. Pre-sales experience drives post-enrolment success and retention.

Recorded lines are the starting point for discipline and meaningful audits. QA spends 1:1 time with ACs through ongoing sessions, and we iterate the process from there.

03 Impact from automation and centralisation

What the six initiatives add up to, and the single number they are all built to move.

1
AI Calling

Brings in revenue from the not-joined leads by reactivating dormant inventory, so recovered revenue from old leads goes up.

2
Futworks Qualification

Frees up agent bandwidth for more productive work. As outsourced calling filters the noise, ACs engage in more qualitative conversations.

3
Sales Best Practices

Trains in the highest and best practices observed across agents, and passes that knowledge across all centres.

4
QA Audits

The most important impact. Over the next 3 to 5 months it brings standardisation to a decentralised business, maintaining pre-sales nurturing quality and instilling discipline.

5
WhatsApp Automation

Improves learner engagement through the pre-sales journey with consistent, timely messaging.

6
Inbound Call Tracking

Gives insight on inbound volume across stores, and how branding and store-level experiments shift inbound call volume pan-India.

Net-net : more revenue from the same leads

Across all these projects, the aim is to improve the lead-to-counsel ratio (L2C by 30%), which translates into a 30% lift in L2P (lead to payment), pulling more revenue from the same volume of leads we already generate.
